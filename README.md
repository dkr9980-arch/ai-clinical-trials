Artificial Intelligence in Clinical Trials
Author: Darshan KR

About the Study

This repository contains my published research paper on the application of **Artificial Intelligence in clinical trial design and patient stratification**. The study focuses on improving efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing outcomes in drug development.


The study critically evaluates:

* AI-driven clinical trial design
* Patient stratification using clinical and molecular data
* Predictive modelling for treatment outcomes
* Adaptive clinical trial methodologies
* Optimization of patient recruitment and cohort selection

Key Concepts Covered

* Clinical trial optimization
* Precision medicine
* Machine learning in healthcare
* Predictive analytics
* Biomarker-based stratification
